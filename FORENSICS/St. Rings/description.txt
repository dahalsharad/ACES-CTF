POINTS: 10
The disappearance of St. Rings, a venerated saint from the Vatican, has sent shockwaves throughout the religious community. The saint was last seen in Crimea, but a search yielded only a cryptic image as the sole clue to their whereabouts. The police are at a loss, and time is running out.

Can you unravel the mystery and find the missing saint before it's too late?
Every second counts in this pulse-pounding race against time to find the truth and bring St. Rings back to safety.
